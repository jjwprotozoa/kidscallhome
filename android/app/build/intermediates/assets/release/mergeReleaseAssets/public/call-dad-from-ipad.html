Call Dad from iPad Without SIM Card

Question: Can my child call me from an iPad that doesn't have a SIM card?

Answer: Yes! Kids Call Home works perfectly on WiFi-only iPads. Your child can make video calls and send messages using just WiFi - no SIM card, phone number, or mobile plan needed.

How to Set It Up

  1. Open Safari on the iPad
  2. Visit kidscallhome.com
  3. Add to Home Screen (creates an app-like experience)
  4. Your child logs in with their special code
  5. They can now call you anytime - no SIM card needed!

Why This Works

  • WiFi-Only: Uses internet connection, not cellular network
  • No Phone Number: Kids log in with a simple code instead
  • Free Forever: No subscription or calling fees
  • Works Anywhere: As long as there's WiFi, they can call

Perfect For:

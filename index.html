Safe video calls for kids — no phone or SIM required

Kids Call Home is a family-only video calling and safe kids messaging app that lets children call parents and family without needing a SIM card or phone number. Works perfectly on tablets, iPads, Kindle Fire, Chromebooks and many other Wi‑Fi devices. Parents control all contacts—no strangers, no public profiles, no random friend requests.

Family-only calls, no strangers

Only parent-approved family members can connect with your child. There is no public profile, no search feature, and no random friend requests. Your child can only communicate with people you've explicitly approved, making it safer than typical kids messaging apps that allow "friends of friends" or public discovery.

Works on tablets, iPads, and Wi‑Fi devices

Kids Call Home works great on tablets, iPads, Kindle Fire, Chromebooks and many other Wi‑Fi devices when kids don't have their own phones or SIM cards. No phone plan or phone number required. Perfect for families who want their children to stay connected without giving them a full smartphone.

Built for co‑parents and long‑distance family

Kids can easily call both parents in different homes and extended family across countries. Ideal for shared custody situations, co‑parenting communication and international families. Parents control which family members are approved, ensuring safe communication across all family structures.

Privacy-first design for kids

Calls and messages are encrypted to protect your family's communication. The app uses minimal data, doesn't use tracking for ads, and does not sell family data to advertisers or partners. There are no manipulative "dark pattern" features like infinite feeds, aggressive notifications, time pressure, or surprise in‑app purchases. Kids Call Home is designed to be a simple, safe communication tool, not a platform for engagement or monetization.

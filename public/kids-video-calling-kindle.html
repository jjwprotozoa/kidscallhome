Video Calling App for Kindle Fire Tablets

Problem: Your child has a Kindle Fire tablet, but most calling apps aren't available in the Amazon App Store.

Solution: Kids Call Home is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works directly in the Silk browser on Kindle Fire tablets. No app store installation required!

How to Use Kids Call Home on Kindle Fire

  1. Open the Silk browser on the Kindle Fire
  2. Visit kidscallhome.com
  3. Tap the menu and select "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Your child logs in with their special code
  5. They can now call you from their Fire tablet!
